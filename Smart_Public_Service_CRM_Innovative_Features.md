# Smart Public Service CRM (PS-CRM)

## Innovative Feature Proposal Document

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. AI-Based Complaint Classification & Auto Routing

-   Automatically categorizes complaints using Natural Language
    Processing (NLP).
-   Assigns priority levels (High/Medium/Low) based on urgency and
    sentiment.
-   Detects duplicate complaints and auto-escalates if multiple reports
    arise from the same location.
-   Performs sentiment analysis to prioritize highly critical or
    emotionally urgent complaints.

## 2. Live Complaint Heatmap (Smart City View)

-   Real-time map visualization showing complaint density across
    regions.
-   Area-wise issue tracking with red-zone identification.
-   Time filter (Last 7 days / 30 days / Custom range).
-   Trend forecasting for potential future problem areas.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. WhatsApp & Voice-Based Complaint System

-   Citizens can register complaints via WhatsApp.
-   Voice-to-text conversion for accessibility.
-   Multilingual support for inclusive governance.
-   Enables digital access for semi-literate and rural populations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Department Performance Scoreboard

-   Displays department-wise performance metrics.
-   Average resolution time tracking.
-   Pending complaint statistics.
-   Citizen satisfaction ratings.
-   Public leaderboard for transparency.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Citizen Trust & Credibility Score

-   Tracks authenticity of user complaints.
-   Identifies and flags fake or spam complaints.
-   Encourages responsible civic participation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Smart Analytics Dashboard

-   Category-wise complaint trends.
-   Monthly and yearly comparison graphs.
-   Resolution time analytics.
-   Top recurring civic issues analysis.
-   State-wise and region-wise breakdown.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Predictive Governance System

-   AI-based forecasting of seasonal civic problems.
-   Predicts waterlogging, electricity shortages, etc.
-   Enables proactive administrative planning.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 8. Emergency Escalation Engine

-   Auto-detection of emergency keywords (fire, accident, collapse).
-   Instant escalation to higher authorities.
-   SMS/Email alerts for urgent cases.
-   High-priority auto-marking for critical mass complaints.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 9. Public Upvote & Community Validation System

-   Citizens can upvote important complaints.
-   Most voted issues appear at the top.
-   Encourages community-driven governance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 10. Gamification for Officials

-   Monthly performance badges.
-   Fastest resolution awards.
-   Incentive tracking dashboard.
-   Encourages accountability and efficiency.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
